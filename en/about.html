Modern Planning Contracting Company

Founded in January 2021, Modern Planning Contracting Company was established in response to high demand in the construction field and market recovery after challenging circumstances. Our aim is to contribute to the ambitious projects recently launched in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

  • We've built a specialized team and formed partnerships.
  • We are specialized in steel structure works, construction, and more.
  • We are committed to meeting local and international standards.

With our team's extensive experience, Modern Planning Contracting Company is committed to meeting the needs and requirements of our clients.
